ACADEMIC CALENDARS AND DATES OF OPENING AND CLOSING EACH TERM

2025-2026 ACADEMIC CALENDAR (TERM ONE)

The school is informing parents and guardians that the 2025-2026 first term opens on 22nd September, 2025. However, all students who have not finished paying school fees will be sent home until the term 3 school fees are cleared. Please, settle the outstanding amount before commencement of the term. This will also apply to students who lost school library book.

For more details about Academic Calendar, see the table below:
