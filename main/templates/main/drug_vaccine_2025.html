UPCOMING MASS DRUG ADMINISTRATION CAMPAIGN FOR OUR STUDENTS

Dear Parents/Guardians,

We are writing to inform you that our school will be participating in the national Mass Drug Administration (MDA) campaign by the Ministry of Health, aimed at promoting the health and well-being of our students. The targeted age group is 9 to 18 years. The campaign is scheduled to run between 27th October to 3rd November, 2025.

As part of the campaign, deworming (praziquantel and albendazole), HPV as well as IFA supplementation medication will be administered to all students.

The campaign is designed to protect our students from parasitic and viral infections and improve their overall health and academic performance. Health professionals from the Ministry of Health will oversee the administration of the medication to ensure everything runs smoothly and safely.

We therefore kindly request your consent or information on any allergies or health concerns for your child/ward before the proposed date of commencement.

If you have any questions or concerns, please reach out to the Headmistress or the Deputy Headmaster.

Thank you for your cooperation.

Sincerely,
E. W. Bonjesi-Mathias
